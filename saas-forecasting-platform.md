# Understanding a SaaS Forecasting Platform: Best Estimates

## Introduction to a SaaS Forecasting Platform

Organizations often rely on expert judgement when predicting uncertain outcomes such as risk exposure, insurance claims, or market trends. A **Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)** platform can help teams manage forecasting activities in a structured and collaborative way.

The Best Estimates initiative by Swiss Re focuses on improving forecasting accuracy by applying behavioural science techniques and structured decision-making methods.

This document provides a simplified overview of how a SaaS-based forecasting platform can support expert predictions.

---

## The Problem SaaS Platforms Aim to Solve

Forecasting often depends on human judgement. Even experienced professionals may produce different predictions for the same scenario.

Two common issues affect forecast quality:

| Issue | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Bias | Systematic errors in judgement caused by assumptions or experience |
| Noise | Variations in predictions between different experts |

A SaaS platform can help reduce these issues by guiding experts through a structured forecasting workflow.

---

## SaaS Platform Workflow for Forecasting

A forecasting platform delivered as a SaaS solution allows experts to collaborate through a structured process.

### Example Workflow

Problem Definition  
↓  
Data Collection  
↓  
Expert Assessment  
↓  
Bias Reduction Techniques  
↓  
Forecast Aggregation  
↓  
Final Prediction Output  

In this workflow:

- Experts define the forecasting question  
- Supporting data is reviewed  
- Individual forecasts are submitted  
- The system aggregates the predictions  
- A final forecast is generated

---

## Key SaaS Features of a Forecasting Platform

A SaaS platform designed for expert forecasting may include the following features:

### Collaborative Forecasting

Multiple experts can submit and review forecasts within the same platform.

### Structured Decision Process

The system guides users through steps that reduce bias and improve consistency.

### Data Integration

Relevant datasets and evidence can be integrated into the forecasting workflow.

### Centralized Forecast Results

All predictions and results are stored and accessible in a single platform.

---

## Benefits of a SaaS Forecasting Platform

Using a SaaS platform for forecasting provides several advantages:

- Improved prediction accuracy  
- Reduced variation between expert opinions  
- Transparent forecasting processes  
- Easier collaboration among teams  
- Centralized data and reporting

These benefits help organizations make better decisions when dealing with uncertainty.

---

## Conclusion

Forecasting complex risks requires both data and expert judgement. A SaaS platform that applies structured forecasting techniques can help organizations reduce bias, improve prediction accuracy, and support better decision-making.
